ABOUT US

Imperial Décor is your luxury event décor and party rental source. We primarily cater the Washington DC, Maryland and Virginia communities for all their event décor needs. In addition, we are also available for travel to any of the neighboring states. We specialize in wedding decorations but also cater to any small or large event. Every design is thoroughly inspected by our structural engineers along with our designers to make sure that the design is not only pleasing to look at but also structurally sound. We believe that attention to details never goes unnoticed and that’s what makes them stand out. In addition to providing Backdrop/Stage designs, Centerpieces/Table décor, we also provide room up-lighting and logistical service to ensure all your event needs are taken care of. Our quality, unique designs and exceptional customer service ensures that your big day will be flawless and without any hitches.
